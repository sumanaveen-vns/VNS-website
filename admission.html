Admissions

We welcome children aged 2 to 5 years. Our admissions are open throughout the year, subject to availability of seats.

Steps to Enroll:

  • Visit our school and meet our staff.
  • Fill out the admission form provided at the office.
  • Submit the required documents (birth certificate, photos, etc.).
  • Pay the admission and tuition fees.
